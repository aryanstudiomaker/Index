GLOW YOUR FACE

Bilkul! Ab main aapko ek poori 1-week routine dunga jisme step-by-step bataya gaya hai:

Kya lagana hai

Kitni der tak lagana hai

Kab dho kar hataana hai

Kya massage karna hai ya chhod dena hai overnight

Sab kuch aapke paas jo cheezein hain unhi se banaya gaya hai. Ye routine dry skin ke liye perfect hai aur glow + dark circles dono ko target karta hai.

DAY 1 (Glow + Start Under Eye Treatment)

Morning:

Cleanse: Raw milk (cotton se poore face par lagaayein, 1 min massage, phir dho lein)

Face Pack:
- Chandan powder – 1 tsp
- Raw milk – 1 tsp
- Honey – ½ tsp
15 mins lagaayein, phir thande paani se dho lein

Night:

Cleanse: Besan + raw milk paste (2 mins massage, dho lein)

Under Eye:
- Potato juice + tomato juice (equal quantity)
- Cotton se lagaayein 10 mins, phir dho lein
- Vitamin E capsule ka tel (½) + honey (1 drop) – massage karke chhod dein overnight

DAY 2 (Mild Scrub + Moisturizing)

Morning:

Scrub: Coffee – 1 tsp, Honey – 1 tsp (1–2 mins massage, dho lein)

Moisturize: Raw milk (dab kar chhod dein)

Night:

Under Eye + Vitamin E + honey (overnight chhod dein)
Optional Mask: Besan + honey – 10 mins, dho lein

DAY 3 (Brightening Haldi Pack)

Morning Pack: Besan – 1 tsp, Haldi – 1 pinch, Raw milk – 1 tsp (15 mins, dho lein)

Night Pack: Chandan + tomato juice – 15 mins (dho lein)

Under Eye: Same + Vitamin E massage

DAY 4 (Hydration Day – No Pack)

Morning: Raw milk cleanse + Vitamin E (1 drop) + Honey (2 drops)

Night: Under Eye: Same | Optional: Chandan + honey – 10 mins

DAY 5 (Scrub + Glow Pack)

Morning Scrub: Besan – 1 tsp, Coffee – ½ tsp, Honey – 1 tsp (2 mins, dho lein)

Night Pack: Chandan + raw milk – 15 mins

Under Eye: Same as above

DAY 6 (Brightening Tomato Pack)

Morning: Tomato juice – 1 tsp, Honey – 1 tsp, Haldi – pinch (10–12 mins)

Night: Besan + milk + honey – 15 mins

Under Eye: Same

DAY 7 (Final Glow Day)

Morning Pack: Chandan – 1 tsp, Honey – 1 tsp, Raw milk – 1 tsp (15 mins)

Night:
- Under Eye: Cold potato juice (10 mins)
- Vitamin E capsule + honey massage (overnight)
- Face Massage: Vitamin E oil + honey (2 min massage, chhod dein overnight)

Reminder Notes:

  • Sab packs 10–15 mins ke liye lagayein.
  • Scrubs sirf 1–2 mins ke liye, halki haath se.
  • Under-eye remedies dho ke Vitamin E + honey laga kar chhod dena hai.
  • Face massage (Day 7) mein sirf halka tel aur honey use karna hai – no heavy oil.
